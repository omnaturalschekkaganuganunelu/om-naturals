# Step-by-Step SEO Action Plan for OM Naturals

It is completely normal that your brand-new website is not showing up on Google yet! Here is why: **Google doesn't know your website exists until it "crawls" and "indexes" it.** For a brand new domain like `www.om-naturals.com`, it can take anywhere from a few days to a couple of weeks for Google to discover it, scan it, and decide where to place it in search results.

Right now, your Instagram, Justdial, and Facebook pages show up because they have existed for a long time and Google already trusts them. 

Since we have already set up your website's **Sitemap, Robots.txt, and Metadata** perfectly, your website is 100% technically ready. Now, you need to manually tell Google to index your site and connect it to your existing online presence.

Here is your exact step-by-step action plan to get your website to the top:

## 1. Link Your Website to Your Google Business Profile (Crucial!)
In your second screenshot, your Google Business Profile (Google Maps listing) is showing up perfectly on the right side, but it says **"Add website"**. This is the single most important step for local searches like "cooking oils near me".

1. Go to Google and search for your business exactly as it appears in the screenshot: `om natural chekka ganuga nune`.
2. Since you own the business profile, you should see buttons to edit it directly in the search results (or go to google.com/business).
3. Click **Edit profile** -> **Contact**.
4. In the **Website** field, enter exactly: `https://www.om-naturals.com/`
5. Save the changes. 
*Why this helps:* Now, anyone who finds you on Maps will click straight to your site. It also tells Google, "This website is the official site for this verified local business."

## 2. Submit Your Site to Google Search Console (Force Google to Crawl)
Instead of waiting for Google to randomly find your website, you can manually submit it.

1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/about) and sign in with your Google account.
2. Click **Add Property**.
3. Choose the **Domain** option and enter `om-naturals.com`.
4. Google will ask you to verify ownership. You will need to copy the TXT record they give you and paste it into your Vercel/Domain DNS settings (the same place you connected your domain).
5. Once verified, go to the **Sitemaps** section on the left menu.
6. Enter `sitemap.xml` and click **Submit**.
*Why this helps:* You just gave Google the exact map of all your products and pages. They will queue it for crawling immediately.

## 3. Update Your Social Media & Directories (Backlinks)
Google looks at how many trusted websites point to your new website. You already have highly ranked profiles!

1. **Instagram**: Edit your bio and add `https://www.om-naturals.com/` to your profile links.
2. **Facebook**: Edit your Facebook page's "About" section and add your website URL.
3. **Justdial**: Log into your Justdial business dashboard and add your website URL.
*Why this helps:* When Google's bots scan your Instagram or Facebook, they will click the link to your new website. This passes "trust" (called link juice) to your new site, boosting its rank.

## 4. Give It Time
SEO is not instant. Once you do the steps above:
- **Within 2-5 days:** Google will start indexing your pages. If you search `site:om-naturals.com` on Google, you will start seeing your pages appear.
- **Within 2-4 weeks:** Your website will start climbing the ranks for your brand name ("om naturals").
- **Over months:** You will start competing for generic keywords like "best cooking oils online".
